Aalu ke Parathe

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Flour
  • Spices

Recipe

  • First - Take some Potatos and boli it for some time.
  • Second - Mean while take some Flour and put some water and knead it.
  • Third - When the potato is boiled, peel it and then smash it, after then add some spices in it.
  • After then make Aalu ka Paratha!!!
